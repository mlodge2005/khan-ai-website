Intel is a digital software product delivered through an install link, download, repository, command, or similar digital access method.

## 2. Setup support guarantee

Khan AI wants customers to get Intel running successfully. If you cannot install or run Intel, contact support at marcus@khan-automation.com within 7 days of purchase.

Khan AI will make a reasonable effort to help you troubleshoot setup issues.

## 3. Refund eligibility

Khan AI may approve a refund if:

- You request support within 7 days of purchase.
- Intel cannot be installed or run on a reasonably supported system after troubleshooting.

Refunds are generally not available if:

- Intel was successfully installed.
- Intel generated a report or analysis.
- You changed your mind after receiving access.
- You no longer want the product.
- You expected a guaranteed business outcome.
- Your AI provider charged usage fees.
- A third-party service changed, blocked, rate limited, or removed data.
- You violated the Terms of Service or Software License Agreement.

## 5. Chargebacks

If there is a billing issue, contact Khan AI first at marcus@khan-automation.com so the issue can be reviewed. Khan AI may revoke access, support, updates, or license rights if a chargeback is filed after product delivery.

## 6. How to request a refund

Email marcus@khan-automation.com with:

- Your name.
- Purchase email.
- Purchase date.
- A short description of the issue.
- Screenshots, logs, or error messages if relevant.

Do not send API keys, passwords, or private credentials.

If a refund is approved, the payment processor controls how long it takes to appear on your original payment method.
